Rupa Dhruv
Producer | Director | Social worker

An educationalist, award winning film maker, social worker, Art of living teacher, core member of Lead India 2020 by former Indian President APJ. 30+ years of experience who devoted her career and impacted 3,00,000 children and beyond.

Rupa Dhruv has produced and directed 15 short films and 2 features. Films…. The journey is still not finished many more to come….3 Films Selected for the screening in the 2 nd Mumbai Shorts International Film Festival, held on 14 th December 2013. One of my film Rashi- Struggle & Beyond won the 3 major awards including the best emerging woman director, Best actress& Best actor awards. The movies have gone to International film festival in New York, Afghanistan Herat Women Voices NowFestival 2014 for the Women suffering in Afghanistan. The filmsare also submitted in Cannes Film Festival.

biography

  • Awarded by Dr APJ Abdul Kalam 2011 as a Best Core Team Member of “Lead India 2020”
  • Awarded Queen Elizabeth citation Award by Mrs Godrej an Award for Oration.
  • Awarded Inter-College for Dramatics writing, Direction and acting
  • Awarded by Shiv Sena as Best Woman Achiever written and Directed 3 skits for Goregaon Malad constituency.
  • Awarded as best trainer for Direction, acting, writing, public speaking by Balkanji Bar
Had an opportunity of serving the less fortunate by training them on Dramatics,Communication Skills, Soft Skills, Oration in Balkan-ji-Bari; Street Plays in hindi and marathi on “Kali Maa ke Das Roop”, “Daruchich Mazza” and “Stri Bruhn Hatya” the hutments in Mumbai for Shiv Sena Youth Wing; Encouraging youth by planning, organising and successfully doing a “Cleanliness Drive” by cleaning the beach; a donation drive for the adivasi children; went to jail for “Anna Hazare” jail bharo in Azad Maidan.
