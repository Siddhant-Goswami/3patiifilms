About Us

'3 Patti Films' is an innovative platform for newcomers and also the experienced ones. As our theme celebrates 'Kal, Aaj aur Kal, we welcome the experienced talent from yester years, fresh creativity of today and innovative ideas of tomorrow. Our name says it all. You may think 3 Patti means gambling and gaming.... yes, we all always gamble with our life and decisions. Here we entrepreneurs have come together to support good talent and give them an opportunity.
